================================================================================
# Materiaroom
================================================================================
Thank you for your interest.

'Materiaroom' is a tool to automatically rename photos.

- Rename to [YYYY-MM-DD_hh-mm-ss.jpg] format.
- And copy files to [YYYY-MM]/[YYYY-MM-DD] folders.

ex)
Copy tom/Desktop/DCIM/R1024854.JPG
-> jerry/2013-03/2013-03-24/2013-03-24_12-34-56.jpg

Let's use 'Materiaroom' if you think

- Where is the simple rename Utility?
- Can't breathe under the unorganized photos.
- Want to merge photos taken with many cameras.

Sincerely,
The Jpico Team
